# Problem Statement

Many SMEs still record sales manually with notebooks, spreadsheets, or calculators. This causes missing records, inventory errors, weak reporting, and difficulty knowing profit or loss.

Traditional POS systems are often expensive, difficult to maintain, and not flexible for small businesses. This project solves the problem by providing an affordable subscription-based POS system with modern business tools.

## Problems Addressed

- Manual sales recording
- Poor inventory tracking
- Lack of affordable POS systems
- Poor sales reporting
- Difficulty managing staff
- Difficulty knowing profit and loss
- Poor customer record keeping
- Lack of multi-branch support
